About Me

Engineering, research, and practical problem solving.

I am a Mechanical Engineering student with a strong interest in robotics, computer-aided design, and engineering simulation. I enjoy transforming concepts into functional systems through analytical design, simulation, and hands-on prototyping.

My projects span 3D printing, embedded NFC systems, finite element analysis, robotic simulation, and autonomous robotics. By combining CAD modeling, computational analysis, and practical fabrication, I aim to develop solutions that are both technically sound and applicable to real-world engineering challenges.

Currently, I am preparing for graduate research opportunities in Japan, with a focus on robotics and advanced mechanical systems.
