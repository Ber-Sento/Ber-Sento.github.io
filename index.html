FEM-Based Gear Contact Stress Optimization using ANSYS Mechanical

Parametric study of gear geometry to minimize contact stress and deformation using FEM simulation.

2026

Conducted finite element analysis of a meshing gear system using ANSYS Mechanical to evaluate contact stress and structural deformation. A parametric study was performed to optimize gear geometry, reducing peak stress and improving mechanical performance. Mesh refinement and geometry simplification were applied to improve computational efficiency without sacrificing critical contact accuracy.
